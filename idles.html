Idles

Idles is a band I have known about since 2017 with the album "brutalism", and is what got me into the postpunk scene even more. I at the time would find new music through youtube suggestions on the right side when watching a video and would just endlessly click on music videos, to find new bands. I really liked the songs "well done" and "mother" and probbably listned to them more than I should have.

Idles has released four full leangth LP's so far with the newest "CRAWLER" being from 2021. Like most punk bands they do cover politics, and topics relating to social stigmas like toxic masculinity. In there entire catalog they have become progressevly more possitive and uplifting, which I admire for a punk band, when ussually they just critisize.
