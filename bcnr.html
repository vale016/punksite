Black Country, New Road

Black country New Road is the band that I have been fond of since there first album "For The First Time". The album is very different from what they make now, with it being a experimental post punk jazz record. Opus and Sunglasses are my favorite songs on the record and have some of the best instramentals derived from, alt rock and Jewish Klezmer instrumentals. After the first record they made there 2nd record "Ants From Up There", which I have listened to probbably over 100 times at this point.

They are a band that you either love like alot, or your indiferent and dont get why there beloved. The album will be remebered fondly to the likes of Slints album Spiderland, and has affected alot of people, including me. After they made the record the lead singer left the band due to decline in mental health, and the band since then has released a live album of new songs, and leans towards alt rock and folk.
