Squid

Squid to me is like Black Midi,in which you either like or you think there God awful. The leader singer being the reason, because of the odd way he sings of pitching his voice up in down. Another intersting thing about the singer is also that he is the drummer and plays while singing. Like anyone that got into them, I heard about them from the single "Narrator", which is nine minute art punk song thats so funky yet horrifyingly good and also just horrifying in the last part of the song with the screeching female vocals.

Squid is a fun and funky band and have only released one album with there second album "Omonolith" coming out in a few months. They like Black Country New Road have inspired alot of other indie bands, and with bands trying to copy what made them popular. There is something so unique and instrumentally intersting about the band compared to alot of what is being produced right now in the indie scene.
