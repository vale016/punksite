black Midi

Black Midi on first listen is probbaly going to put you off. The first time I heard a song from them I found the composition to be too spiratic and also didn't like the lead singers voice. Now since that first time listening to them, its been over a year and a half and there one of my favorite bands.

There really such a unique band taking elements from punk, alt rock, jazz, prog rock, and avante garde. Each member holds there own and plays as fast as they can. The drummer is incredbly underlooked and is a phenomenal drummer who rivals drummers such as Keith Moon. While the band is not for everyone they do have songs that are more palatable like: Still, The Defence, and Asending fourth, that are much slower tracks that are also grand in scale and layered instrumentally.
