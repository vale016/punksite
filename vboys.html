Viagra Boys

Viagra boys is a band the was formed in 2015, when the leader singer Sebastian Murphy was drunk with his friends. He was singing Karoke and his friend told him they have to form a band, and now a couple years later they are a sucessfull punk band.They have three albums out: Street Worms, Wellfare Jazz and Cave World. They are a unique sounding band, using fusions of punk, like dance, post, and hardcore.

What I like about the band is bassically everything, they utilize sax, humor, political commentary, unique vocals and instrumentals. With how fun the lyrics are you just can help but sings along with songs like: Sports, Slow Learner, Baby Criminal, Frog Strap and ect just being so fucking good songs. Bands Like Viagra Boys are what I love about the post punk scene right now.
